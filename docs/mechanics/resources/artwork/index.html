    Artwork and Design

    Artwork and Design

    The general art style will be comic like as for example World of Warcraft. This helps the game to get fast assets as its easier then archiving foto-realism and also these styles are more timeless.

    Links

    Every game needs a good amount of artwork and assets. To make it easier to find and integrate new components some links where assembled.

    If you want to integrate new assets into Bestia it is important to chose some with an appropriate licence. Since the game itself and currently the assets are released under a free licence the artwork licence must at least allow the bundling and sharing. Creative Commons or Public Domain is preferred.

    • Bitmap Fonts (most of them Public Domain). Has a nice gallery of bitmap fonts freely available.
    • Time Fantasy A real nice asset of compatible tilesets and sprites for RPG style games. Mostly they are free.
    • Game-Icons.net A lot of usable icons with a CC BY 3.0 licence.
    • Kenney Big chunk of assets some of them free to use.
    • itch.io A lot of nice game assets. Some are free, and some can be bought for a few Dollars.
