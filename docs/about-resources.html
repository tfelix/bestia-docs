Resources

Resources are raw materials used to sell to other players. They can be used to create or build items or buildings. Resources can be created by the following means:

  • Killing of enemies
  • Gathering of natural resources in the game world
  • Using special extracting buildings or devices
  • Recycling of existing items

Dropped items in the world are semi-permanent. The player have a strong incentive to gather dropped items to participate in the market.

Types of resources

The resources in the world of Bestia a basically one of these types:

Type Vorkommen Usage
Ores, Minerals, Crystals Mountains, Caves Raffinery, Alchemy, Magic, Blacksmiths
Mana Whole world, can be extracted more easily on hotspots Magic Use
Herbs Whole world, especially forrests Magic Use & Alchemy
Wood Forrests Building material
Stone Mountains Building material
Salts Mountains or Sea Magic Use & Alchemy, Cooking
Wheat, Vegetables, Berries Farmland, Woodlang Consumables and Cooking

Item Recycling

For each item is a list of base resources which the item is roughly based upon (this list is also used when forging or creating the item). When an attempt to recycle the item there is a chance that the item is destroyed and the resources are lost.

The base chance for item recylcing is:

Item Lv. Base Success Chance
1 - 20 65%
20 - 60 45%
> 60 25%

The chance can be altered by players INT (slightly) and DEX and also by the apropriate skill.

If successfull performed a recycling attempt the amount of captured resources is determined:

Item Lv. Base Resource Reclaim
1 - 20 50%
20 - 60 40%
> 60 30%

Also this chance is altered by the resource reclaim skill as well as INT (slightly) and DEX.
